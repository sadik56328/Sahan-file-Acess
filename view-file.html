Faylka Caafimaadka ee Bukaanka

Bogga Hore

Macluumaadka Bukaanka

Magaca: Maxamed Cali

Taariikhda Diiwaangelinta: 2025-06-29

Baritaan (Diagnosis)

Bukaanku wuxuu leeyahay calaamado xanuun caloosha ah oo joogto ah. Waxaa lagu sameeyay ultrasound.

Daawooyinka (Medications)

  • Omeprazole 20mg - Maalin kasta 1 tablet
  • Paracetamol 500mg - Marka xanuunka uu bato

Waxyeelada (Side Effects)

Omeprazole wuxuu sababi karaa madax wareer iyo matag haddii la isticmaalo si xad dhaaf ah.

Wacyigelin (Awareness)

Fadlan la tasho dhakhtar ka hor intaadan bedelin daawo. Cun cunto caafimaad leh iyo cab biyo badan.
